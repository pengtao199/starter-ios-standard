# Tier Selection Reference

## Lite

Use for:

1. Utility apps
2. Small single-purpose apps
3. Projects with few screens and low integration cost

## Standard

Use for:

1. Subscription apps
2. Productivity apps
3. Most projects that need Settings, Support, Legal, and Paywall from day one

## Pro

Use for:

1. Media-heavy apps
2. Cache/download-heavy apps
3. Long-lived products with stronger architectural governance

Default recommendation: `Standard`
